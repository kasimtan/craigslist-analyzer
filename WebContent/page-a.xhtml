If the tags like h:form and h:commandButton are totally ignored, it probably means that you don't have jsf-api.jar and jsf-impl.jar in WEB-INF/lib, or that you haven't mapped the FacesServlet in web.xml. If you get a JSF-specific error message, it probably means that you used the wrong URL (i.e, ending in page-a.xhtml instead of page-a.jsf).
